Privacy Policy for PocketPath

Effective Date: 29/01/2025

1. Introduction

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how PocketPath handles user data and ensures your information remains private and secure.

2. Information We Collect

PocketPath does not collect, transmit, or share personal data externally. All data is stored locally on your device in an SQLite database within the app’s internal storage.

Locally Stored Data:

  • Expense records (e.g., transaction details, categories, amounts, dates).
  • User preferences & settings.

This data remains on your device only and is not sent to any server, third party, or cloud storage.

3. How We Use Your Data

Since PocketPath stores data locally, we use it only for:

  • ✔ Tracking expenses and managing records within the app.
  • ✔ Generating and exporting financial reports (CSV/PDF) for user convenience.

4. Data Sharing & Security

  • We do not collect, share, or sell your data.
  • Your data is stored securely only on your device.
  • The app does not use external servers, cloud databases, or third-party analytics.

Data Sharing via Expo Share

PocketPath allows users to export and share their expense data as CSV or PDF files via third-party apps (e.g., email, messaging apps, cloud storage).

  • Sharing is user-initiated—the app does not send files automatically.
  • Once shared, the user is responsible for the security of the exported file.

5. Your Rights & Control Over Data

You have complete control over your data. You can:

  • ✅ Delete your data by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app.
  • ✅ Export your data (CSV/PDF) and share it manually.
  • ✅ Manage your sharing preferences within your device settings.

Since the app does not store data externally, we do not have access to retrieve or modify your data remotely.
